Mark Goodrick

Mark Goodrick writes stories of mystery, imagination, and the hidden truths that shape every life, including the Olivia Turner mystery series.

Mark Goodrick is the author of the Olivia Turner mystery series. A lifelong reader of crime fiction and suspense, he began writing his own stories after retiring from a career in management. His work focuses on layered mysteries, psychological tension, and characters determined to uncover the truths others would rather keep hidden.

Goodrick lives in Winters, Texas, where the quiet rhythms of small‑town life often remind him that every place holds stories waiting to be discovered. Through the Olivia Turner series, he follows a determined private investigator whose instincts and persistence lead her into cases filled with hidden motives, buried secrets, and the complicated truths people try to conceal.

In addition to the Olivia Turner mysteries, Goodrick has written several other suspenseful works, including House of Evil, The Last Call, The Shatterer, and the short story collection 10 Around the Campfire, with additional short story collections planned. When he is not writing, he enjoys playing golf, hunting, fishing, watching football, and spending time with his family, who remain one of his greatest sources of encouragement and inspiration.

At the heart of his writing is a simple belief: the best stories explore mystery, imagination, and the hidden truths that shape every life.

The Shatterer

Detective Ryan Cole is enmeshed in the gloom of rain-drenched Drayton City, chasing the enigmatic killer known as The Shatterer alongside his partner Rita Lawrence. Engulfed by the city's dark undercurrents and battling personal demons, Cole dives deep into its secrets, determined to find justice. As alliances form and the past resurfaces, the pursuit becomes a complex dance with morality, challenging the lines between right and wrong. The hunt tests loyalties and forces Cole to face his own shadows and former ally Emma Holloway, whose path has darkly intertwined with his own. This is a tale of unyielding resolve amid the city's sinister heartbeat, where every step toward the truth is one further into the abyss of personal redemption.

Olivia Turner and the Case of the Vanishing Heiress

When a terrified woman vanishes during a violent storm, private investigator Olivia turner can't shake the feeling she's just witnessed the beginning of something far darker. The missing woman is Clara Ashworth, reclusive heiress to one of Hollow Bay's most powerful families. Summoned to the Ashworth estate, Olivia finds a house full of locked wings, erased records, and carefully staged lies - all pointing to a disappearance meant to look voluntary. But as Olivia and her investigative partner, Riley Cruz dig deeper, they uncover signs of psychological manipulation, forced confinement, and a legacy built on silence. Every clue leads back to the fog-shrouded mansion perched on the cliffs, where someone is determined to keep the truth buried - and to discredit anyone who gets too close. Atmospheric, suspenseful, and psychologically charged, Olivia Turner and the Case of the Vanishing Heiress is a gripping mystery about truth, power, and the cost of uncovering secrets that were never meant to surface.
